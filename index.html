Open News Digest

Open News Digest is a news service that fetches article details from various
news sources. Search capabilities on news source websites are often poor and
often don't get you what you were looking for. This app is API driven and not only
retrieves article details, which allow you to decide if the article is worth reading
but it allows you to email the article details including a direct link to the article itself
to yourself or someone else. It is built with AngularJS to handle the front end and
uses an API service built with Ruby on Rails to create and send emails.
